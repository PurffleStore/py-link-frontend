ABOUT PY-LINK

What is Py-Link?

Py-Link is a content tool that helps professionals stay updated in their field and share useful information with their network.

Built by Pykara Technologies

What Py-Link does

Py-Link checks the websites and sources you choose. It reads new articles and updates from those sources. Then it prepares a short summary and ready-to-use post drafts based on what it found.

The results are sent to your email. You can read the summary, review the drafts, make any changes and post when you are ready. Nothing is posted automatically.

Why we built it

Many professionals want to stay informed about their field. But reading many websites and writing posts takes a lot of time. Most people do not have that time every day.

Py-Link was built to solve this problem. It does the reading and writing for you so you can stay active and informed without spending hours on it each week.

Who uses Py-Link

Py-Link is useful for any professional who wants to keep up with their field and share useful updates with their network. This includes:

  • Software engineers and technology professionals
  • Doctors, healthcare workers and medical professionals
  • Lawyers, consultants and finance professionals
  • Business owners and entrepreneurs
  • Anyone who wants to stay informed and share knowledge

How it works

Step 1 — Pick your sources

Choose the websites and feeds you want to follow. Set a time range for how far back to look.

Step 2 — Run Py-Link

Click Generate. Py-Link checks your sources and finds useful updates from that time range.

Step 3 — Get your results

You receive a short summary and post drafts by email. Review them and post when ready.

Step 4 — You are in control

Nothing is posted without your action. Edit any draft or ignore it. You decide what goes out.
