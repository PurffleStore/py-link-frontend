WHAT IT DOES

Stay informed in your field.
Share what you know. All in one place.

Tracks websites from your field

You add the websites you want to follow. It checks them regularly and collects new and useful updates so you do not have to visit each website yourself.

Gives you knowledge for your role

You set your profession and interests. It reads the same websites but picks only what is useful for your role. A person in a different field will get different results from the same websites. This way you gain knowledge that is actually relevant to you.

Sends a short summary to your inbox

For each useful update it finds, it prepares a short summary explaining what happened, why it matters and how it is useful for you. You get this in your email every day. No need to log in or open any website.

Prepares social media posts for you

Along with the knowledge summary, it also prepares post drafts for LinkedIn, Facebook and Twitter. You can choose how you want the posts to sound and it will follow that style every time.

Hashtags included with every post

For every post draft it prepares, it also gives you ready hashtags that match the topic. You do not have to think about which hashtags to use. Just copy and add them when you post.

Suggests an image idea for each post

Along with the post, it gives a short description of what kind of image will go well with it. You can use that with any image creation tool. It does not create the image but gives you a clear idea of what to make.

HOW IT WORKS

How it works

1

Set up once

Create your profile. Add your profession, role and interests. This is your persona. It uses this to understand what kind of updates are useful for you. Then add the websites you want to follow and set how often you want updates.

2

It does the work

It reads all your selected websites and filters the content based on your persona and role. It picks only what is useful for your profession, prepares a short summary, post drafts, hashtags and image ideas and sends everything to your email.

3

You just post

Open your email. Read your updates. Pick the posts you like, edit if needed and share on LinkedIn, Facebook or Twitter when you are ready.

FAQ

Common questions

Here are some answers that may help you before you start.

Does it only help with posts or does it also help me learn?

It does both. For every useful update it finds, it prepares a short summary that explains what happened, why it matters and how it is relevant to your profession. You can read this to stay informed about your field even if you do not want to post anything. The posts are optional. The knowledge update comes first.

How does it help me stay updated in my field?

It reads all the websites you have selected and picks only the updates that are relevant to your profession and interests. Every result comes with a short summary so you can quickly understand what is new without reading the full article. Over time this helps you stay informed about what is happening in your field without spending hours every day.

How does it know what is useful for me?

When you set up your profile, you choose your professional role and the topics you follow. It uses this to filter what it finds. Two people can track the same websites but get different results because their roles and interests are different. It shows each person only what is relevant to them.

Do I need any technical knowledge to use this?

No. It is very simple to use. You pick your sources, set a time range, enter your email and run it. The whole setup takes about two minutes. You do not need any technical background.

Which sources can it check?

You add the website URLs you want to track. It can be company blogs, industry news sites, research portals, product update pages or any public website. You can add as many sources as you need and turn them on or off anytime.

Where do I receive my results?

The results are sent to your email. You will get a short summary and ready-to-use post drafts. Each draft includes the original article link so you can check the source before posting. You do not need to log in to read the results.

Can I choose how often it runs?

Yes. You can set it to run daily, weekly or at a time that suits you. You can also run it manually whenever you want. You can also choose how far back it should look, such as the last 24 hours, 48 hours or 72 hours.

Can I change how the posts sound?

Yes. You can choose the style you prefer such as professional, conversational or analytical. It will use that style each time it prepares content for you. You can always edit the drafts before posting.

Which platforms does it support?

It supports LinkedIn, Facebook and Twitter/X. For each platform the post is prepared in a different way. LinkedIn posts are more professional. Facebook posts are more general. Twitter/X posts are short and to the point. You can choose which platform you want before each run.

What is the image idea it gives for each post?

For each post it prepares, it also writes a short description of what kind of image would work well with that post. You can copy this description and use any image creation tool to make a suitable image. It does not create the image itself, but gives you a clear idea of what to create.

What if I am not happy with a draft?

You are always in control. You can edit any draft, rewrite it or ignore it. Nothing is posted without your action. If you want better results, you can change your sources or tone settings and run it again.
